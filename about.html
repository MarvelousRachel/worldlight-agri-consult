About Us

We empower farmers and aquaculture entrepreneurs worldwide with innovative agricultural and aquaculture machinery, backed by expert consultancy. Our team combines graduate-level expertise in feed, nutrition, and production systems with advanced robotics and automation technology to bridge cutting-edge technology with practical agricultural solutions.

Our Team Expertise

Our team brings together world-class specialists combining advanced technology with deep nutritional science:

  • Robotics & Automation Technology: Advanced expertise in intelligent equipment automation, industrial robotic arm design, and control systems
  • Aquatic Animal Nutrition & Feed Technology: Specialized research in aquafeed formulation, fish nutritional physiology, and molecular nutrition techniques
  • Advanced Manufacturing: Assembly line automation, injection molding machines, and robotic arms with humanoid hand technology
  • Nutritional Science Research: Dietary immunomodulation, oxidative stress, antioxidant defense, and nutrient-gene interactions
  • Sustainable Aquaculture: Comprehensive understanding of fish health, growth optimization, and sustainable feed development
